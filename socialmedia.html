SOCIAL MEDIA

Over 3+ billion uses social media but not a lot of people go into their privacy settings to check What access they give. T hat is why l'll be showing you what access you give by default and what you can disable/enable.

What does Facebook do with my data?

The majority of this information is used for marketing purposes. Facebook collects information on what you buy, what your friends buy, the groups you join (to see your interests) , shows you ads that the company thinks you may like.

Which apps did I give permission to collect my data?

To check this follow the following steps:

Step 1:

Go to Settings

Step 2:

Select Settings & Privacy

Step 3:

Select Apps & Websites

Step 4:

Either click View and edit to see what data each app collects or Remove the apps you don't need anymore.

Step 5:

Last but not least, make sure to do the FB Security Checkup to make sure your account is up to date with the most important settings such as 2 Factor Authentication, Login alerts & a Strong password

How do I disable my location for Facebook?

It's very easy. Go to the Settings on your phone, then navigate to Location → Apps → Permissions and choose for yourself which apps may know your location.

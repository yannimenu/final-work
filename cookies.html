COOKIES

Cookies are small files sent to your browser from websites you visit. These files then track and monitor the sites you visit and the items you click on these pages. Research shows that over 85% of all cookies on the internet are not secured, all the more reason to learn how to protect them.

Purpose

Cookies remember the products you've clicked on, the locations you've checked frequently. types of stories you are interested in, and even your login information. They create a personalized internet experience to streamline your searches

Accepting cookies

When you accept cookies, the website can get info about:

• The amount of time you spend on e website
• The links you click while using the website
• The options, preferences or settings you choose
• Accounts you log into
• Recording which pages you've visited in the past
• Items in a shopping basket

Solution 1

The first solution to make sure thet cookies are deleted the moment you leave a certain website is through the settings of your browser:

Step 1:

Go to Settings

Step 2:

Click Show advanced settings

Step 3:

Click Content settings

Step 4:

Under Cookies, select Keep local until you quit your browser

Step 5:

Click Done
